BDCOM

Reliable Networking for Critical Infrastructure

We deliver BDCOM networking solutions approved for Qatar infrastructure projects. With local support, fast delivery, and engineering expertise, we ensure seamless execution.

BDCOM is a globally recognized provider of advanced networking solutions, specializing in high-performance switching and wireless technologies. In Qatar, BDCOM switching solutions are approved by MOI-SSD and listed under Ashghal AVL, making them a trusted choice for consultants, contractors, and system integrators involved in critical infrastructure projects. In addition to switching, BDCOM offers a comprehensive range of wireless (WiFi) solutions designed for enterprise and commercial environments, ensuring reliable and high-speed connectivity. All BDCOM solutions are locally supplied and supported by CADD Arabia Qatar, providing clients with fast delivery, expert technical guidance, and dependable after-sales support.
